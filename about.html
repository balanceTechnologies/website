We Are BalanceTech

Balancetech comprises a passionate assembly of students from Gebze Technical University, united in our pursuit of excellence within the Ball on Plate project. Our primary objective revolves around implementing and perfecting PID algorithms to ensure the precise balance of the ball. Going beyond mere stability, we've innovated by creating a comprehensive desktop application for virtualization and a user-friendly mobile app, empowering intuitive control of the system. Our collaborative efforts aim not just for success in this endeavor but also to contribute valuable insights and advancements to the realm of control systems and technology.
